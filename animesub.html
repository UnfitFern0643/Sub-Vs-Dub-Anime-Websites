"Subbed" anime is anime with the original VAs (Voice Actors), meaning all the characters speak Japanese, but there is subtitles on the bottom of the screen so people that don't speak Japanese can understand what they're saying.The 1st ever anime created for audiences with the original VAs that was shown in America was called Toppu o Nerae! (which translates to "Aim for the Top!" in English), also known as "Gunbuster". Toppu o Nerae!! aired from the 7th of October in 1988 up until the 7th of July in 1989, and was translated by "U.S. Renditions"> from March of 1990.
